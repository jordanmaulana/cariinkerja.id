Why It DefendsMore than a wrapper

A vertical pipeline, built for this market.

01

Structured scoring pipeline

Every score is schema-validated (Pydantic) — not free-text. A cheap relevance gate runs first, the full model only on real candidates. Reliable, idempotent, retried.

02

Local-first by design

Indonesian job boards, Bahasa "kamu" verdicts, IDR pricing, LinkedIn-native onboarding. Built for the market, not translated into it.

03

Open source & auditable

AGPL-3.0. Users can verify how scoring works and how their data is handled — trust as a feature, not a promise.

04

Shipped & operating

Full async crawl-and-assess infrastructure already in production, running daily for paying users. The hard part is built.
